# Review: Three Consecutive Odds

## Solution
- **Approach**: Linear scan with a counter that increments for odd numbers and resets for even numbers, returning true when the counter reaches 3.
- **Time Complexity**: O(n)
- **Space Complexity**: O(1)
- **Correctness**: Correct
